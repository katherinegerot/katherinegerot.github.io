Nebraska Local Technical Assistance Program

$AC:AREAEX:Home Banner PLC$

The mission of Nebraska LTAP is to enhance the skills of local and state transportation professionals in providing a safe, efficient, environmentally sound transportation system in Nebraska

Training

Nebraska Local Technical Assistance Program provides training to meet the needs of township, city, village, county and state, and transportation professionals, consultants, and technicians.

We offer a wide range of workshops and online certification courses that are designed specifically to improve performance, build technical skills, and contribute to professional development in the field of transportation. Training sessions are led by subject matter experts (SMEs) with extensive experience in their fields.

Events

Nebraska Local Technical Assistance Program hosts and supports a variety of regional and statewide conferences that offer opportunities for road professionals to share experiences, ideas, and knowledge. Conference subjects range from technical in nature (Bridge conference) to skills competition (Winter Maintenance Workshop).
